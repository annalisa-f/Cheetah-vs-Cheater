Episodes

Episode One

When Peter the cheetah learns that his friend Propaganda the panda has been captured and put in a zoo, he ventures out to save him. But on his journey through the gardens he encounters a money-hungry business man chopping down all the trees, who won’t let him through. Peter goes through anyway, and a chase ensues.

Episode Two

During a busy afternoon rush hour, the businessman and Peter the cheetah continue their chase. Faced with obstacles along the way, the businessman uses a taxi to catch up to an ever victorious Peter where the chase reaches a high outside the grounds of New Zealand's parliament. The business man finds himself stuck in the rush hour traffic once more, and Peter makes a run for it - but has he got away?

Episode Three

The businessman continues the chase along the Wellington waterfront, where Peter is able to evade him long enough to ask for directions and take a dip in the ocean. Peter’s break from pursuit goes wrong however when the pollution in the ocean causes him to fall ill, and almost gets captured by the businessman.

Episode Four

Peter the Cheetah is lost on Cuba St after his map is blown away by the Wellington wind. While the businessman takes a break to eat a burger, the cheetah desperately searches for it. He finds the map just as the businessman finishes the burger, and makes it across the pedestrian lights in time to avoid the businessman.

Episode Five

Almost at the Zoo, Peetah wanders through Massey University and the man in the suit gets ever closer. He is so close to reaching his friend he can almost see him. But car smog, library labyrinths and overwhelming vending machines get in his path. Peetah manages to make it out of the college just in time, heading straight for the zoo.

Episode Six

Peter successfully arrives at Newtown zoo and tries to locate Propaganda, unknowingly attracting the attention of the zookeeping staff, all the while the businessman is hot on his trail. Peter manages to steal a key and free Propaganda, just as the zookeepers and the business man catch up to him.
